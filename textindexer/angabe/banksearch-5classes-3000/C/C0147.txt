	Period of Insurance 
	Twelve months from the date of commencement of this Insurance and any subsequent period for which the Insured shall pay and the Insurer shall agree to accept a renewal premium. 
	
	
	Whereas the Insured has applied to the Insurer for the insurance hereinafter contained and has paid or agreed to pay the premium as consideration for such insurance.
	
Now this policy witnesseth that subject to the terms, exceptions and conditions herein or endorsed hereon at any time during the initial insurance period or in any subsequent period in respect of which the Insured shall pay to the Insurer and it shall accept the premium required for the renewal of this insurance, if the Property (or any part thereof) shall be lost, damaged or destroyed anywhere in the world by accident or misfortune, the Insurer will replace the Property or at its option otherwise indemnify the Insured.

3. In the event that any item insured under this Policy is lost, damaged or destroyed, the Insurer will pay the cost of repair or replacement as new provided that:
	
	
- no cash will be paid in lieu of repair or replacement of the item
	
	
- the amount payable in respect of a claim for any individual item will not exceed the sum insured for such item shown on the Schedule of Equipment
	
	
- the amount payable in respect of a claim for an item acquired second-hand or which has been designated as old or rare, will be limited to the cost of replacing that item by one of similar age and condition.

	Conditions
	
	
- This Policy of Insurance and the Schedule shall be read together as one contract and any word or expression to which a specific meaning has been attached in any part of this Policy or of the Schedule shall bear such specific meaning wherever it may appear.
	
- The Insured shall take all reasonable precautions for the safety of the Property.
	
- The Insured shall be resident in the United Kingdom and the Property shall normally be kept in the United Kingdom unless otherwise agreed. The Insured shall notify the Insurer prior to departure on any journey outside the United Kingdom which is expected to last for more than three weeks. The Insurer may as a consequence impose special terms or decline to provide cover for the duration of that journey.
	
- The Insured shall, as soon as is reasonably practicable after the happening of any loss or damage to the Property give notice thereof in writing to the Insurer before repairers are instructed and shall at his own expense deliver to the Insurer a claim in writing with such details, particulars and proofs as may be reasonably required and shall take all practicable steps for the recovery of any article claimed for. In the event of theft or accidental loss, immediate notice shall be given to the Police.
	
- If any claim shall be in any respect fraudulent or any fraudulent means or devices are used by the Insured or anyone acting on his behalf to obtain any benefit under this Policy all benefits thereunder shall be forfeited.
	
- If at the time of loss, damage or destruction there shall be any other insurance covering the same loss, damage or destruction the Insurer shall not be liable to pay or contribute more than the rateable proportion of such loss, damage or destruction.
	
- The Insurer shall be entitled if it so desires to prosecute in the name of the Insured for its own benefit any claim for indemnity and shall have full discretion in the conduct of any proceedings and the Insured shall give such information and assistance to the Insurer as it may require.
	
- The Insurer may cancel this Policy by sending notice by recorded delivery letter to the Insured at his last known address and in such event the Insured shall become entitled to the return of a proportionate part of the premium corresponding to the unexpired period of insurance. A notice sent by recorded delivery letter in pursuance of this condition shall be deemed to be received at the time it should have been delivered in the ordinary course of post.
	
- If any difference shall arise as to the amount to be paid under this Policy (liability being otherwise admitted) such difference shall be referred to an arbitrator to be appointed by the parties in accordance with the statutory provision in that behalf for the time being in force. Where any difference is by this condition to be referred to arbitration the making of an award shall be a condition precedent to any right of action against the Insurer.
	
- The due observance and fulfilment of the terms, provisions and conditions of this Policy in so far as they relate to anything to be done or complied with by the Insured shall be conditions precedent to any liability of the Insurer to make any payment under this Policy.
	
- Any mis-statement concealment of any material fact, mis-representation or mis-description in any material particular renders this Policy void.
	
- This Policy is not transferable. 
	
- The Insured shall notify the Insurer immediately of any change of address at which the Property is normally kept and shall provide details of security precautions at the new address.
