 Complete Computer Cover is a specialist policy, designed specifically 
 to ensure you are not left without computing power for long if your machine 
 should be lost, stolen or damaged. Efficient claims handling enables swift 
 repair or replacement of equipment.
 
This 
 policy covers 'all risks' with no excess; 1 years cover; includes 
 laptops, memory boards and standard software. Simple premium calculation 
 based on the value of your equipment. Valid claims are approved within 
 24 hours and replacement equipment obtained without delay.
 
	
	
	 
	
	
	
 
 Covers computer hardware (including laptops, printers and boards) plus standard 
 software for all risks, including theft from a locked car boot. 
	
	
 
 
	
	
	
 
 Exclusions are limited e.g. it does not cover thefts from unattended cars where the item 
 was not hidden from view and where equipment has been left in the open unattended. 
 Electrical and mechanical breakdown are not covered. For a full list of exclusions see the policy details.
