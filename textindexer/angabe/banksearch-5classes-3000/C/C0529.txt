
Christian Bookshop Insurance
 
 
 
 
 This policy is designed
 particularly to meet the essential needs of Christian Bookshops.
Buildings and Contents Cover includes:
 
- 5% No Claims Discount
 
- 30% Seasonal Stock Increase
 
- Public & Products Liability - £2,000,000
 
- Employees' Personal Effects - £500
 
- Business interruption up to 12 months
 
- Money (£2,000 during business hours)
 
 
Recognising the special needs of Christian Bookshops,
 Connect offers:
 
- Personal Accident cover for employees and voluntary workers
 
- Cover for book displays in churches and other exhibitions
 
- Goods in Transit
 
- Low excess of £75 for Buildings, Contents and Money
 
- Legal and Emergency Helplines
