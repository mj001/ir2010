	Schedule 
	most recent schedule issued to the Insured by the Insurer 
	
	
	Territorial Limits 
	United Kingdom the Channel Islands and the Isle of Man 
	
	
	Insuring Clause
	If any Property is lost destroyed or damaged by any accidental cause during the Period of Insurance and within the Territorial Limits the Insurer will indemnify the Insured for such Damage by replacement to the original prescription 
	
	
	Basis of Payment
	In the event of loss or destruction of or damage to Property the amount payable shall be the cost of replacement or repair to the original prescription
	If a claim should arise for one lens of a pair of lenses insured under this Policy the amount payable shall not exceed 50% of the Sum Insured for the pair of lenses
	The liability of the Insurer under this Policy shall not exceed the Sum Insured stated in the Schedule
	 
	
	
	
	 
	Policy Exclusions
	
This Policy does not cover
	
	
- Damage due to
	
	
- war invasion act of foreign enemy hostilities (whether war be declared or not) civil war rebellion revolution insurrection or military or usurped power
	
- 
	
	
- ionising radiations or contamination by radioactivity from any nuclear fuel or from any nuclear waste from the combustion of nuclear fuel
	
- the radioactive toxic or other hazardous properties of any explosive nuclear assembly or component thereof
	
	
	
- the first part of the cost of any claim being 8% of the Sum Insured for hard lenses and 10% of the Sum Insured for soft lenses
	
- Damage when the Property is not being worn unless kept in the special container supplied by the Insured&#146;s optician
	
- Damage as a result of manufacturing defect scratching discoloration protein accumulation wear and tear or any natural or gradual deterioration
	
- Damage arising from any process of cleaning or restoring
	
	 
	
	
	 
	Conditions
	
	
	
	
- Procedure for Notifying Claims
	If any event happens which may give rise to a claim being made under this Policy the Insured shall as soon as possible and in any event not later than seven days thereafter give notice to the Insurer of the potential claim The Insured shall at their own expense complete and deliver to the Insurer a claim form giving particulars of the circumstances of Damage together with any other documentation as shall be reasonably required by the Insurer Unless otherwise agreed by the Insurer the completed claim form and other documentation must be received by the Insurer within three months of the date of Damage

- Fraud
	If any claim shall be in any respect fraudulent or any fraudulent means or devices are used by the Insured or anyone acting on their behalf to obtain any benefit under this Policy all benefits thereunder shall be forfeited
	
	
	
- Duty of Care
	The Insured shall take all reasonable precautions for the safety of the Property
	
	
	
- Other Insurance
	If at the time of Damage there shall be any other insurance covering the same Damage the Insurer shall not be liable to pay or contribute more than a rateable proportion of such Damage
	
	
	
- Insurer's Rights
	The Insurer shall be entitled if it so desires to prosecute in the name of the Insured for its own benefit any claim for indemnity and shall have full discretion in the conduct of any proceedings and the Insured shall give such information and assistance to the Insurer as it may require
	
	
	
- Cancellation
	The Insurer may cancel this Policy by sending seven days notice by recorded delivery letter to the Insured at their last known address and in such event the Insured shall become entitled to the return of a proportionate part of the premium corresponding to the unexpired period of insurance A notice sent by recorded delivery letter in pursuance of this Condition shall be deemed to be received at the time it should have been delivered in ordinary course of post
	
	
	
- Arbitration
	If any difference shall arise as to the amount paid under this Policy (liability being otherwise admitted) such difference shall be referred to an arbitrator to be appointed by the parties in accordance with the statutory provisions in force at the time Where any difference is by this Condition to be referred to arbitration the making of an award shall be a condition precedent to any liability of the Insurer to make any payment under this Policy
	
	
	
- Policy Provisions
	The due observance and fulfilment of the provisions of this Policy in so far as they relate to anything to be done or complied with by the Insured shall be a condition precedent to any liability of the Insurer to make any payment under this Policy
	
	
	
- Misrepresentation
	Any misstatement concealment of any material fact misrepresentation or misdescription in any material particular renders this Policy void at the Insurer&#146;s option
	
	
	
- Reinstatement of Sum Insured
	The Sum Insured shall not be reduced in the event of a claim under this Policy subject to Condition herein
	
	
	
- Declinature
	The Insurer reserves the right to decline to reinstate or renew this Policy if three or more claims have occurred during any period of twelve months
	
	
	
- Tax
	In addition to the premium the Insured shall pay to the Insurer any tax due on the premium which the Insurer is required to collect in accordance with current legislation
	
	
	
- Law
	There is a choice of law applicable to this insurance but unless agreed otherwise between the Insured and the Insurer English Law will apply
