 Browser 
 and Monitor Compatibility
 
 
This 
 site is designed to support Internet Explorer 4.0, Netscape 
 Navigator 4.0 and all later versions.
 
 This site has been optimally designed to be viewed at a resolution 
 of at least 800x600. For instructions on how to reset your 
 monitor's resolution to 800x600 or higher (if you are currently 
 using a resolution that is lower than this) please consult 
 your monitor's handbook or refer to its manufacturer.
