Making a Claim
 
If your equipment is lost or damaged, please follow the 
 procedure below:
 
 
- Call the Call the Police if the computer has been lost or stolen or 
 damaged by thieves and obtain a crime reference number.
 
- Make sure that you have 
 
 
- Your policy number
 
- The processor serial number 
 
- A description of the fault symptoms
 
- a crime reference number (if applicable)
 
 
 
- Call Burnetts on 023 8044 4345 
 
- Retain any damaged property or parts
