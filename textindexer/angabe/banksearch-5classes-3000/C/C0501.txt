Ewen Gilmour is a chartered accountant and has worked 
 in the Lloyd&#146;s market for seven years. He joined the Chaucer 
 Group in March 1998 and is Managing Director of Chaucer Holdings 
 PLC and Chaucer Syndicates Limited. He was formerly a corporate 
 financier with Charterhouse Bank from 1980 to 1993, becoming 
 a director in 1987. He joined the Corporation of Lloyd&#146;s 
 to help facilitate the introduction of corporate capital to 
 the Lloyd&#146;s market in 1993. He is also a member of the 
 Lloyd's MSU Board.

Robert Stuchbery is a Fellow of the Chartered Insurance 
 Institute and has worked in the Lloyd&#146;s market for 13 years. 
 He was appointed Underwriting Director of Chaucer Syndicates 
 Limited in April 2000, responsible for overseeing all underwriting 
 within the Group. He was previously the active underwriter of 
 Syndicate 1096. Prior to joining Lloyd&#146;s he was with CNA 
 Reinsurance of London Limited, working both in London and the 
 USA. He is an elected council member of the Lloyd&#146;s Non-Marine 
 Association.

 Hugh Hayward

Ted Noble is a chartered accountant and held senior 
 positions with two insurance companies prior to his appointment 
 in 1989 as Finance Director of MFK Underwriting Agencies Limited. 
 Following the acquisition of MFK, he became Syndicate Finance 
 Director and latterly Finance Director. He was also Compliance 
 Officer from late 1996 until April 2000. In September 2000 he 
 was appointed as Group Finance Director of Chaucer Holdings 
 PLC, whilst continuing as Finance Director of the managing agency. 
 Ted is a member of the LUAA Managing Agents' Finance Committee.

Simon Boome

 in the law firm of Cameron Markby Hewitt in 1994. His areas 
 of expertise include Company Law and Corporate Finance. Simon 
 was appointed as a Non-Executive Director in January 1997 and

Chris Forbes 
 
Chris Forbes is a qualified Barrister and Chartered Accountant. 
 His involvement in the Lloyd's market began in 1981 and in 1991 
 he was appointed Finance Director of Charman Underwriting. Chris 
 joined DP Mann in the position of Finance Director, in 1992 
 and was appointed Managing Director in 1996. During his time 
 in Lloyd's he has served on a number of committees and groups, 
 including the Board of Additional Securities Limited and the 
 Lloyd's North American Board.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Malcolm Cox
 
 Malcolm Cox joined Syndicate 990 in 1968. During his 30 
 years at the syndicate his experience spanned many areas, including 
 reinsurance advice, claims settling and underwriting. He has 
 experience in all territories of the world and has underwritten 
 all classes of non-marine business, direct and reinsurance, 
 and aviation reinsurance. 
 
Malcolm served as the Active Underwriter for Syndicate 
 990 for 18 years. In January 2000, he resigned as Active Underwriter 
 of Syndicate 990 and was appointed as Chief Executive Officer 
 and Underwriting Director of Denham Syndicate Management Limited.
 
He has served on many Lloyd's committees 
 and groups, including the Lloyd's Underwriters' Non Marine Association 
 of which he was the Chairman in 1998.
