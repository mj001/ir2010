The cost of cover
 
 The cost of cover after the FREE COVER period will depend 
 on the amount of monthly cover you need and the type of 
 cover you select.
 
Premiums for cover under this policy are collected monthly 
 in advance by direct debit. Your first premium will become 
 due in the month immediately following the FREE COVER period. 
 
 
Monthly premium rates 
 
The costs of cover are expressed as a rate per £100 of 
 monthly benefit and include Insurance Premium Tax.
