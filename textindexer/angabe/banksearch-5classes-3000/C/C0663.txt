Home Insurance - Contents cover policy
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The meaning of words 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
If we explain what a word means, that word has 
 the same meaning wherever it appears in your 
 policy or schedule. 
 
 These words are highlighted in bold throughout 
 your policy. We have defined the most 
 common words below. They do not apply to:
 
 
- section 4a - Home legal advice; and 
 
 
- section 4b - Home legal protection. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 We, 
 us, our 
 Cornhill Insurance PLC. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 You 
 , your 
 the person named as the policyholder 
 in the schedule, their partner and members of their 
 family permanently living with them at their home address 
 shown in the schedule. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Schedule 
 a printed document showing the sections 
 of the policy you have chosen, the sums insured 
 and any special terms that apply to your policy. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Unfurnished 
 not having enough furniture to live in 
 permanently. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Unoccupied 
 not having been lived in for more than 
 30 days in a row. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Endorsement 
 changes to the terms of your policy 
 which will be shown in your schedule or continuation 
 schedule. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Policy 
 the contract of insurance between you 
 and us. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 We explain other words 
 at the beginning of each section.
