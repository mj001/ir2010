 Complaints procedure 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
If you are not 
 satisfied with the way our Customer 
 Centre has handled your question or 
 you are not happy with the answer and 
 want to complain please write to our 
 Customer Services Manager.
 
We will contact 
 you within 5 working days of receiving 
 your complaint to tell you 
 what we are doing. We 
 will try to sort out the problem and give 
 you an answer within 4 weeks. If it 
 will take us longer than 4 weeks we 
 will tell you when you 
 can expect an answer.
 
If we have not 
 given you an answer within 8 weeks 
 we will tell you how 
 you can take your 
 complaint to the Financial Ombudsman 
	Service.
