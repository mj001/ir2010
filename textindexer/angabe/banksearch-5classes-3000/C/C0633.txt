Home Insurance - Contents cover policy
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 General exceptions 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Applying to all sections 
 of this policy (but not to 4a and 4b) 
 
 
 
 This policy does not 
 cover the following: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 1) 
 Loss, damage, injury or liability as 
 a result of war, revolution or any similar event. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 2) 
 Loss, damage, injury or liability arising 
 out of anything which happens outside Great Britain, Northern 
 Ireland, the Isle of Man or the Channel Islands except 
 as shown in sections 2, 3 and 4. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 3) 
 
 
Loss or damage or any legal liability directly or indirectly 
 caused by: 
 
 
- ionising radiation or contamination by radioactivity 
 from any nuclear fuel or from any nuclear waste from 
 burning nuclear fuel; 
 
 
- the radioactive, toxic, explosive or other dangerous 
 properties of any explosive nuclear machinery or any 
 part of it; or 
 
 
- pressure waves caused by aircraft and other flying 
 objects.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 4) 
 
 
Loss or damage:
 
 
- resulting from theft or attempted theft by you; 
 
 
 
- resulting from a deliberate act by you, your 
 domestic staff or any tenant, lodger or paying guest; 
 or 
 
 
- you suffered as a result of being deceived 
 into knowingly parting with your property.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 5) 
 Loss of market value after an item is 
 repaired or replaced. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 6) 
 We will not pay for damage caused 
 by or resulting from pollution or contamination, other 
 than damage caused by: 
 
 a) pollution or contamination 
 which results from damage by a cause which is insured 
 by this policy; or 
 b) damage by a cause which is insured by this policy 
 which results from pollution or contamination.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 7) 
 Loss, damage, injury or liability as 
 a result of an event which happened before the cover under 
 this policy started.
