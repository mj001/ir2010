
Guardian Financial Services
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Guardian Financial Services provide 
 a service to over a million customers from our office 
 in Lytham St Annes, Lancashire.
 
Over 800 people work in the Guardian organisation. 
 
 
We value interpersonal skills highly and teamwork is 
 central to our working practices. 
 
Our people are enthusiastic, self motivated and actively 
 seek solutions to problems. A flexible approach enables 
 our staff to adapt to and enjoy change. 
 
Our work performance is measured regularly and we are 
 rewarded with: 
 
 
 
 
 a good salary and related benefits 
 
 
 
 
 career opportunities 
 
 
 
 profit share when targets are met 
 
 
 
 
 family-friendly policies such as flexible 
 working hours 
 
 
 
 a pleasant and friendly working environment. 
 
 
 
 
As an Investor in People, we provide direction, training 
 and development opportunities for all our staff and 
 offer support and encouragement for their self development.
 
We believe that our company behaviour, driven by shared 
 values, will enable us to deliver the high standards 
 of service which our customers expect in their relationship 
 with us. These values are:
 
Helpful 
 to all customers and colleagues
 
Self-assured 
 as a successful, quality company
 
Fast-acting 
 to take decisions quickly
 
Imaginative 
 in finding solutions
 
Straightforward 
 to make sure information is clear and customers understand 
 situations
 
Expert 
 to help people fulfill their changing financial needs
