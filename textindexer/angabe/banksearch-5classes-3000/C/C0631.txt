 Marine Syndicate 1084 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Hugh Hayward 
 has worked in the Lloyd&#146;s market for 14 years. He was appointed 
 active underwriter of Syndicate 1084 in October 1988. Prior to 
 that, he spent 10 years with Folksam International (UK) Insurance 
 Company Limited where he rose from Deputy Marine Underwriter to 
 Marine Underwriter. From 1966 to 1978 Hugh was with the River 
 Thames Insurance Company, where he became Deputy Hull Underwriter. 
 He additionally has specific responsibility within Chaucer Syndicates 
 Limited as Aviation Director.
 
 Contact 
 Hugh Hayward 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Tim Howard-Smith was appointed Deputy Underwriter of 
 Syndicate 1084 in 1992. He was with Syndicate 401 of Cuthbert 
 Heath Underwriting Agencies Limited from 1980 as an Assistant 
 Underwriter rising to Deputy Underwriter in 1988. Prior to this 
 he was an Underwriting Assistant with a Lloyd&#146;s syndicate 
 in 1979 and gained initial training as a broker with Willis Faber 
 and Dumas Limited from 1978-1979. Tim is a recognised Hull leader 
 and is also the syndicate&#146;s compliance representative. 
 
 
 Contact 
 Tim Howard-Smith
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Chris Wood was appointed Aviation Underwriter in October 
 1998. He joined Sturge Aviation in 1973, began underwriting 
 in 1977 and became a Director in 1979. He became Deputy Underwriter 
 of Syndicate 925 in 1984 and has written all classes of Aviation 
 business. 
 
Contact Chris 
 Wood
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Peter Wakefield joined Syndicate 1084 in 1998 as Deputy 
 Aviation Underwriter. Prior to joining the syndicate he was 
 with L A Hudson Syndicate 925 from 1987 to 1998, apart from 
 1991 to 1993, when he was with Aviation Syndicate 960. Prior 
 to that he was with Willis Faber Underwriting Management (Aviation) 
 from 1977 to 1987. 
 
Contact 
 Peter Wakefield
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Tony Judd is responsible for writing the Energy account 
 for Syndicate 1084. He joined Syndicate 16/17/18 in 1982, assuming 
 responsibility for the Energy account in 1983. Prior to joining 
 the syndicate he was a broker, with his initial training at 
 Sedgwick and then with a specialist broking house where he was 
 a Marine Energy broker. Tony is a recognised lead of Energy 
 risks, and served on the Joint Rig Committee for twelve years. 
 
 
Contact Tony Judd
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Chris White is the Deputy Energy Underwriter, a position 
 he has held since 1995. He previously worked at St. Katherine 
 Insurance Company, where from 1983 to 1987 he was an Underwriting 
 & Claims Assistant in the Marine & Aviation Division. 
 Subsequently he moved to Syndicate 185 as an Assistant Underwriter. 
 In 1990 he joined the Cuthbert Heath managed Syndicate 401 before 
 transferring to Syndicate 1084 in 1992.
 
 Contact Chris 
 White
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Philip Graham joined Syndicate 1084 in April 2000 as 
 International Reinsurance Underwriter. He joined CIGNA in 1989, 
 moving to Paris in 1993, where he was Deputy Marine Underwriter 
 for CIGNA France. From 1995 until he joined Syndicate 1084, 
 Philip was Worldwide Cargo Underwriter with CIGNA in London.
 
 Contact Philip 
 Graham 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Simon Le Mare was appointed as the Cargo and Specie 
 Underwriter for Syndicate 1084 in January 1994. Prior to this 
 he had been with Syndicate 65 since 1981 where he rose from 
 Assistant Underwriter on the Cargo account in 1986, to Cargo 
 and Specie Deputy Underwriter in 1990 and finally taking responsibility 
 for the account in 1992. Simon is a recognised Cargo leader 
 and is an elected member of the Joint Cargo Committee, and the 
 Technical and Clauses Committee. 
 
 Contact Simon 
 Le Mare
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Nick Kilhams joined Syndicate 1084 in 1998 to assume 
 responsibility for writing the Political Risks account. Prior 
 to joining the syndicate, Nick was an Underwriter of Political 
 Risks for AIG in London. Nick started working in the London 
 market as a broker in 1991. He participated on the Graduate 
 Training Scheme at Willis Faber and Dumas Limited before specialising 
 in Political Risks, firstly as a broker and then subsequently 
 as an underwriter at AIG in 1996. 
 
Contact Nick 
 Kilhams
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Tony Gates joined Syndicate 17 in 1990 as Deputy Claims 
 Manager and was appointed Claims Manager in 1992 and has continued 
 in this position for Syndicate 1084. Prior to this he was with 
 Syndicate 697 from 1979, joining first as an Underwriting Assistant 
 and was subsequently promoted to the position of Claims Manager 
 in 1988. Tony is a representative on the LUA Marine Claims committee.
 
 Contact Tony Gates
