
Charity Shop Insurance
 
 
 
 
 This policy is designed
 particularly to meet the essential needs of Charity shops.
Cover includes:
 
- 5% No Claims Discount
 
- 30% Seasonal Stock Increase
 
- Public & Products Liability - £2 million
 
- Employees' Personal Effects - £500
 
- Business Interruption
 
- Money - £2,000 during business hours
 
 
Charity Shops have become an essential fundraising
 mechanism for many worthy causes. Increasing competition, with the ensuing quality
 improvements of both stock and presentation, necessitate the need for adequate insurance
 cover:
 
- Cover for donated stock
 
- Low excess of £50 for Buildings, Contents and Money
 
- Personal Accident cover for all voluntary workers and
 employees
 
- Legal and Emergency Helplines
