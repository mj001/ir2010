Directions 
 To PoundGates Ipswich Office
 Click 
 Here To View/Print a Street Map of the Area. 
 
 
 
- 
 From 
 A12/A14 Intersection (Copdock)
 
 
 
 
- 
 Take 
 A14 signposted "Felixstowe"
 
 
 
 
- 
 Take 
 first exit signposted "Ipswich Docks" and follow all signs 
 to Ipswich Docks/Ipswich Central
 
 
 
 
- 
 Your 
 route will take you along Wherstead Road, follow signs for Town Centre 
 until you approach Stoke Bridge.
 
 
 
 
- 
 At 
 Stoke Bridge you will have the Novotel ahead of you.
 
 
 
 
- 
 Head 
 towards the Novotel and at the final roundabout before the Novotel exit 
 right into the one way system, signposted "Felixstowe" and 
 "Docks" but keep to the right hand lane.
 
 
 
 
- 
 Eventually 
 you will come to a T-Junction with traffic lights where traffic joins 
 you from the left, this is the access into Grimwade Street.
 
 
 
 
- 
 Follow 
 traffic to the right at these lights but immediately switch to the left 
 lane after turning.
 
 
 
 
- 
 The 
 entrance to Sorrel Horse Mews is approximately 200 yards on the left 
 before the next T Junction and directly opposite the Salvation Army 
 building.
 
 
 
 
- 
 Sorrel 
 Horse Mews is indentifiable from the road by the "Public House 
 Style" sign of a gold horses head on a black background and the 
 striking cranberry colour of the corner office just past the entrance.
