
Special Events
 
 
 
 
 
 The Special 
 Events policy is a flexible package designed for charities, 
 church groups, non-profit organisations and voluntary groups 
 involved in organising or exhibiting at indoor and outdoor events.
 
 Activities include barbecues, bazaars, bring and buy sales, 
 car boot sales, concerts, conferences, craft exhibitions, dances, 
 exhibitions, fetes, fun runs, fundraising events (excluding 
 hazardous activities), garden parties, joint church activities/celebrations. 
 jumble sales, seminars, shows, sponsored walks etc. 
 
 The policy provides public and products liability cover that 
 is essential when organising events for the general public
 
 
Outline of cover
 
- Public Liability (COMPULSORY SECTION)
 
- Indemnity limit of £1,000,000 any one claim (higher limit
 available).
 
- Legal liability for damages, legal costs and expenses as a
 result of injury to the public or damage to their property.
 
 
Optional sections
 
 
- All risks
 
- Cancellation Expenses
 
- Employers' Liability
 
- Money
 
- Personal Accident
 
 
Features
 
 
- 5% No Claims Discount
 
- 5% Registered Charities Discount
 
- Legal and Emergency Helplines
 
- Discounts for 3 or more sections 
 of cover
