TOP TEN TRAVELLERS TIPS
 FROM THE FOREIGN OFFICE (UK)
 
 1. Check the Web site www.fco.gov.uk/knowbeforeyougo 
 2. Get Travel
 Insurance and check the cover is adequate
 3. Research and learn about local laws and customs
 4. Ensure you have a valid passport and necessary visas
 5. Get vaccinations at least six weeks prior to departure
 6. Check http://www.doh.gov.uk/traveladvice
 for health tips
 7. Make sure your holiday is ABTA or ATOL bonded
 8. Leave copies of your passport, insurance etc with family/friends
 9. Take enough money, plus emergency travellers' cheques or dollars
 10. Give a copy of your itinerary & contact details to
 friends/family
