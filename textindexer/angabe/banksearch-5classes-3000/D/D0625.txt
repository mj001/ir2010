Fade
Text that fades in and out.
FadeFree
A functional demo that fades between different lines of text.
Fader
Fade between any number of images. This applet is highly configurable and each image can have it's own hyper-link! 
Fader
A cool little applet that fades text in, scrolls up and fades out as
new text is entering from the bottom. Very easy to configure and it's
Free!
FaceMutator
A completely Java-based line drawing morpher. Source included!
FadeMenu
The free FadeMenu applet displays animated hyperlinks that fade in and out of view. As the author says, it's a "cool navigation toy for your homepage."
FadeScroll
A scrolling applet that fades the text in and out at the edges of the screen.
Has a nice shadow effect and configurable colors and fonts.
FadeRedLight
If you're looking for a super-fast loading applet (only 4K!) to spice up your site, the free fadeRedLight applet may be perfect for you.
Fading Messages
Fading Messages fades in and out an unlimited number of custom formatted
messages with associated URL links.
Fading News
Display your messages with this cool app that
fades text in... and out... and in....
Fan
Expands shaded text horizontally.
FastBanner
Finally. A banner you can control ... link to any site, show your own graphics.
Favicon
This awesome tool allows you to create and save the 16x16 favicon used in the favorites directory, location bar, and taskbar.
Figurines
A clean image animator that allows multiple animations to be started and stopped by the user. All source is included!
FindIt
This is a really cool applet that presents you with two identical (almost)
images. Find the differences and click on them before the clock runs out
and you get to move on to the next level.
FindIt 2
You're faced with two identical images, (almost). Are you up to the
challenge of finding the differences before the clock runs out? (Major update of FindIt applet above)
FineDots
FineDot is a message presentation applet with a dissolve transition effect.
Every message can be linked to a URL. Configurable colors, font types, font
styles, font sizes and the transition effect.
FineMenu
FineMenu V1.0 is a new "Roll Over" menu. Menu items can scroll and support multiple links!
fire
Fire is another free visual applet you can use to heat up your Web site. And of course, source code is included!
Firefirst
The free Fire applet is customizable; you can change the color of the fire, the message, the speed, the timing of the flame, etc.
FireWorks
Run a fireworks show behind your site title with this handy little applet that accepts a text parameter! Source included!
Firework
Generates an attractive fireworks display.
fireworks
A configurable fireworks simulator. Source included!
fishy
If you'd like to have a fish tank but don't want the hassle, check out this free fishy applet--and no feeding is required!
Flag
This applet will animate a user-defined image to make it appear as though it is being blown in the wind.
Flag
Make your image appear as though it is being blown in the wind like a flag.
Flame
We've all seen flames before, but this one's hot! This free applet features a configurable message that goes up in flames.
Flow
Get your gifs wet, and then watch them flow away with the water! A really cool image transition effect!
FontDisplay
The free FontDisplay applet enables you to visualise the effects of fonts in different styles, colors, background colors, and sizes before using them in your applications. The colors are shown in the RGB (Red , Green, Blue) pattern you select.
fontSelect
The free fontSelect applet is for use within your applets and applications, and allows users to choose the best text display.
Fountain
A very configurable animated text display applet.
Fractal Generator
An interactive applet which displays the standard Mandelbrot Fractal and allows the
user to scroll around and zoom in and out at will.
framer
The FREE framer provides a button-over description of each button, and can be customized to be any row or column size.
FreeData
FreeData is a searchable database for a web site without any need for server-side CGI support.
FreeText
Increase the amount of information you display on your Webpage without increasing the clutter. Display many pages of information in just one area of your webpage and give your webpage a dynamic animated feel.
FS
Scrolls multi-colored text in a variety of directions.
fxText
Scrolls a tiled background through the transparent areas of a foreground image.
