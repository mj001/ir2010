We charge fees in 
 the circumstances described below. All fees, unless otherwise specified, 
 will be charged to your account with us. We may alter our fees from time 
 to time, make new fees, or change the manner in which fees are charged, 
 for any one or more of the following reasons:
 
 
(a) where the fees 
 are altered to your advantage;
 (b) to reflect the cost of providing services or facilities that are 
 available on your account, including any changes arising from inflation;
 (c) if the services or facilities on your account are changed;
 (d) to take account of any new legislation, or to comply with an appropriate 
 code of practice or the decision of any court, ombudsman, regulator 
 or similar body;
 (e) to enable us to improve our efficiency;
 (f) because of changes in technology or general commercial practice;
 (g) in response to general market conditions;
 (h) in response to the actions of our competitors.
 
 
Details of any changes 
 will be advised to customers and will be available in our offices before 
 the changes are implemented.
