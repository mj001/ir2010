What is the Two Year Term Share?
A Two year investment account paying increasing interest as
your balance grows.
Withdrawal:
Partial withdrawals are allowed, but a penalty of
180 days interest will be charged.
Minimum investment: £2,500
Maximum investment: £250,000
Interest:
Paid on the 1 December each year. Calculated on a
daily basis from the date we receive your
investment up to the date of withdrawal.
Our staff will be
delighted to open an account for you.
