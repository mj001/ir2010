 Job
 Title: Financial Consultant 
 Grade:
 
 
 
 Department:
 
 Reports
 to: Area Manager/ Operations
 Manager/Head of Financial Services 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Main
 purpose of job
 
 
 
 
 
 
- 
 
To achieve individual
 sales targets through leads generated by the
 branch staff across the field of regulated
 products.
 
 
- 
 
To act with due diligence
 and care and to represent both Leeds &
 Holbeck Financial Services Ltd. and Leeds
 & Holbeck Building Society in a fit and proper
 manner with utmost professionalism.
 
 
- 
 
To maintain Key
 Performance Indicators in line with
 regulatory requirements.
 
 
- 
 
To attend all necessary
 training and achieve Competency with in the
 regulators guidelines.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Key
 Accountables
 
 
 
 
 
 
- 
 
Developing the leads
 provided by the branch team.
 
 
- 
 
Carry out regular periodic
 reviews of your clients in line with
 "best advice" guidelines.
 
 
- 
 
To develop both mortgage
 related and general insurance leads for the
 branch staff.
 
 
- 
 
Gain referrals from
 clients for potential new business.
 
 
- 
 
To strive to achieve
 production targets as set by the Company.
 
 
- 
 
You will be required to
 attend all relevant training as required by
 Norwich Union in order for you to fulfill
 your role of Financial Consultant.
 
 
- 
 
You will be responsible
 for your own Compliance issues and be
 expected to maintain your KPI’s in
 accordance with company requirements.
 
 
- 
 
In accordance with
 "Developing Excellence" your line
 manager will do all within his powers to
 ensure your personal development is
 maintained and that you will be given
 opportunities to develop your career further
 with the Company.
 
 
- 
 
You will be expected to
 attend the Branch Training session on a
 regular basis and have positive input into
 that session.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Internal
 and external relationships
 
 
 
 
 
Internal: Branch
 colleagues, Retail Sales Manager, Branch
 Managers, Head Office Departments, Development
 Managers.
 
External: Members
 & Customers, Norwich Union and Professional
 Connections, Future Mortgages.
 
 
 
 
 
 
Responsibilities
 for Resources, Financial/Human/Physical
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
- 
 
You will be expected to
 update all relevant literature as and when
 required by Norwich Union and the Society.
 
 
- 
 
To present information to
 customers in a clear, precise and
 understandable manner.
 
 
- 
 
Carry out your role as
 Financial Consultant in accordance with the
 Norwich Union Selling Practices.
 
 
- 
 
Provide cover for your
 Financial Consultant colleagues in the event
 of sickness or extended leave at their place
 of work.
 
 
- 
 
To maintain accurate
 records at all times and to ensure that in
 your absence, those records are kept secure
 and confidential.
 
 
 
- 
 
Be aware of the
 "complaints procedure" in
 accordance with the regulators guidelines.
 
 
- 
 
In accordance with
 "developing excellence" you will
 be expected to strive towards achieving the
 Core Competencies for non management staff.

 Skills & Experience
