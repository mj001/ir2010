References required
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Employed: employer&#146;s reference 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Self employed: to 85% LTV = accountant&#146;s 
 certificate covering last 2 yrs trading (full trading accounts

Self employed: to 95% LTV = accountant&#146;s

All mortgages held must be disclosed
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Existing lender&#146;s reference 
 required for all mortgages held including applicants residential 
 property &#150; please enclose a cheque/s made payable to 
 the existing lender/s for this reference (refer to individual 
 lender for charge). Alternatively, latest original lender 
 annual statement. If annual statement is dated more than 3 
 months prior to application, please additionally submit either 
 an original letter from lender addressed to applicant/s confirming 
 most recent 3 months payments made, or alternatively, last 
 3 months full bank statements confirming same 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Clear credit bureau search (ie. 
 no CCJs/defaults etc)
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
FTB: most recent 3 months bank statements 
 for each applicant and these must be consecutive
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Other requirements
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
First time buyer: only available 
 to Self employed if Self cert option chosen
 
 
	 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Available to employed/self employed 
 and contract workers
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Property to be let on assured shorthold 
 tenancy for a maximum of 12 months 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Certificate of rental value from 
 an appropriately qualified person. This certificate will be 
 issued by Verso on application 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Property to be let through a reputable 
 agent 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Employed: all applicants must have 
 been employed for a minimum of 6 months immediately prior 
 to the application being made
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Contract workers: all applicants 
 must have a minimum 12 month contract with at least 6 months 
 left to run 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Applicant/s must be UK or EU nationals 
 and be resident in the UK and in employment. Non UK or EU 
 nationals may be considered, please refer for further details 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Where the applicant/s is not on 
 electoral roll at current address, the applicant/s will be 
 required to submit proof of address in the form of either 
 a utility bill, drivers licence, credit card statement or 
 letter from bank and these must be originals 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
All applications will be subject 
 to a credit score assessment
