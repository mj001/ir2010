References required
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Existing lender&#146;s reference 
 required for all mortgages held, please enclose a cheque/s 
 made payable to the applicants existing lender for this reference 
 (refer to lender for charge) 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Alternatively, latest original lenders 
 statement, together with most recent 3 months full bank statements 
 should be supplied NB: Where applicant/s own several properties, 
 which are subject to a mortgage, please refer to Verso in 
 the first instance
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Clear credit bureau search (ie. 
 no CCJs/defaults etc)
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Where applicants do not have an 
 existing mortgage or are first time buyers: most recent 3 
 months full bank statements for each applicant and these must 
 be consecutive
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Other requirements
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
First time buyer: only available 
 to Self employed if Self cert option chosen
 
 
	 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Assured shorthold tenancy for a 
 maximum of 12 months
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Only 1 tenancy per property
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
No CCJs, DSS tenants, company lets, 
 tenants with diplomatic immunity or multi-let properties will 
 be considered
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Applicant/s must be UK or EU nationals 
 and resident in the UK and in employment
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Verso reserve the right to insist 
 on the appointment of a suitably qualified letting agent 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Although the property is to be let, 
 the principal use of the property must be residential owner 
 occupier and this must be confirmed by the valuer who must 
 also confirm that there is a good demand for a) sale on an 
 owner-occupier basis and b) letting on an assured shorthold 
 basis.The valuer must also confirm that the property is in 
 a suitable condition to be let. The rental valuation will 
 be assessed on the basis of a single &#147;family unit&#148; 
 letting on an unfurnished basis
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
No commercial properties, ex-local 
 authority flats, ex Ministry of Defence properties, ex housing 
 association properties or specially adapted properties or 
 properties with special or restricted uses will be considered
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Location of property: England, Scotland 
 and Wales only 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Comprehensive property insurance 
 is required, applicant/s solicitors may act for Verso providing 
 they are on the Verso panel 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
All applications will be subject 
 to a credit score assessment
