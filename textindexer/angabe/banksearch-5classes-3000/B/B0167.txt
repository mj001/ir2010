 * AER. The Annual Equivalent Rate, which is a notional rate illustrating

 Premium) will be paid by cheque. Withdrawals of £60,000 or more 
 will take a couple of days to process. The minimum cheque withdrawal is 
 £25 unless stated otherwise in the product leaflet.
