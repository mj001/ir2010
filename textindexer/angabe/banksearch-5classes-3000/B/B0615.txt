 Product Description
 
 A 1.25% discount 
 off the standard variable rate currently 
 5.70% (APR 5.90%) is available for advances upto 80% LTV. A 1.25% 
 discount off the standard variable rate currently 5.90% (APR 6.30%) 
 is available for advances from 81% up to 95% LTV.

LTV
 (Loan to Value)
 
LTV is the amount you want to borrow expressed 
 as a % of the purchase price or valuation of the property, whichever 
 is the lower. E.g. If the purchase price and valuation is £100,000 
 and you want to borrow £95,000 this would be 95% LTV.
