	Rainbow Instant Access
	
Product Benefits
	
	
- Instant access with no loss of interest.
	
- Several tiers of interest, the more you invest, the higher the interest rate.
	
- Interest accrues daily and is paid annually on 31st December.
	
- Monthly interest option available, on balances over £1,000.
	
- Interest can be paid gross (with no tax deducted) to non-tax payers. Subject to the completion of Inland Revenue from R85.
	
- Standing Order facility available.
	
	
Further Information
	
	
- Minimum investment subject to the limits defined by the Society at the time of application.
	
- Gross means the contractual rate of interest payable not taking account of the deduction of income tax at the appropriate rate.
	
- Net rates are illustrative only and allow for deduction from gross rate of income tax at the current rate of 20%.
	
- AER stands for Annual Equivalent Rate and illustrates what interest was paid and added each year.
	
- Interest is normally paid at the net rate other than if the Account-holder is entitled to receive interest gross or the account is exempt from Income Tax.
