Premier Monthly
 
What is Premier Monthly?
It is a 30 day notice a/c which pays increased interest with a
growing balance. It allows one free withdrawal a
year.
Withdrawal: 30 days notice - no interest penalty
Minimum investment: £25,000
Maximum investment: £250,000
Interest: 
 Paid monthly on the first day of each month, paid direct into a
bank account. It cannot be added to the account. Calculated on a
daily basis from the date we receive your
investment up to the date of withdrawal.
Our staff will be delighted to open an account for you.
