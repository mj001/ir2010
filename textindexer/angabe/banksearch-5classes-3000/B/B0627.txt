General Standards Insurance Council (GISC)
	
 TMB is an Insurance Intermediary and a member of the
 General Insurance Standards Council, an independent
 organisation which was set up to regulate the sales,
 advisory and service standards of Members (Insurers,
 Intermediaries (including brokers), agents and anyone
 acting for them). Its main purpose is to make sure that
 general insurance customers are treated fairly.
