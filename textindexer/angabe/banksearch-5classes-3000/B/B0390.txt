Key
 Connect Household
 Policy
 
Full policy details can
 be provided on request. Brief details of the areas covered are as
 follows:
 
 
 
 
BUILDINGS
 
The home and its
 decorations, fixtures & fittings, drives, patios &
 terraces, gates & fences, tennis courts.
 
 
 
 
CONTENTS
 
Household goods,
 personal belongings and clothing within the home.
 
 
 
 
 
 
Standard
 Buildings Cover includes
 accidental damage to sanitary ware, fixed glass and double
 glazing, solar panels & ceramic hobs. Accidental damage
 to underground services.
 
 
 
 Standard
 Contents Cover includes accidental
 damage to TVs, audio and visual equipment, radios, home computers
 & video players all situated within the home. Also cover for
 pedal cycles up to £250 as standard. 
 
 
Optional
 Contents Cover includes accidental
 damage, cover for valuables & personal posessions away from
 the home, money & credit card cover, freezer cover, pedal
 cycles cover away from the home. 
 
 
 
 
The following perils are
 covered
 
 
 
- Fire, smoke lightning,
 explosion or earthquake
 
- Storm or flood
 
- Escape of water or oil
 from fixed water tanks, apparatus or pipes
 
- Water freezing in any
 fixed domestic water or heating installation
 
- Theft or attempted theft
 
- Collision by vehicle or
 animal, aircraft and other flying devices
 
- Riot, violent disorder,
 strike, labour disturbance, civil commotion or malicious acts
 
- Subsidence, heave or
 landslip
 
- Breakage or collapse of
 fixed radio, TV aerials and satellite dishes
 
 
Falling trees, branches,
 telegraph poles or lamp posts
