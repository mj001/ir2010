
Darlington Building Society
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 'Pool 
 Supporter Savings Account 
 
 
 1. 
 
 The minimum investment is £1. The maximum investment is £500,000 
 subject to the maximum aggregate retail investment holding with 
 the Society not exceeding £500,000 per individual. If the Account 
 is held on a joint basis, the maximum investment remains £500,000 
 per Account. Investments and withdrawals can be made at any 
 Branch or Agency, subject to Branch or Agency withdrawal limits. 
 
 
 
 2. 
 The 
 Account is available only to individuals and may not be used 
 for business banking purposes. 
 
 
 3. 
 The 
 interest earned on the Account varies according to the balance, 
 i.e. the interest rate on the whole balance increases or decreases 
 automatically as the capital balance moves between the bands 
 which change at £500 and £5,000. 
 
 
 4. 
 Interest 
 is calculated on a daily basis and is credited to your Account 
 on 30th June. 
 
 
 5. 
 Withdrawals 
 may be made without notice or penalty at any Branch or Agency, 
 subject to Branch or Agency limits. 
 
 
 6. 
 
 The Society may at any time decide not to accept further monies 
 into the Account (apart from interest credited) and may close 
 the Account without first giving you notice. If the Account 
 is closed by the Society under this condition, the Society will 
 immediately pay to you the amount of any credit balance in the 
 Account, together with a sum equivalent to the interest which 
 would have been due to the Account up to the date of closure 
 at the appropriate rate of interest, less any charges due to 
 the Society. 
 
 
 7. 
 
 The 
 Society reserves the right not to accept applications to open 
 accounts from people residing outside the Society's operating 
 area. 
 
 
 
 These 
 Terms and Conditions should be read in conjunction with our 
 General Terms and Conditions (Savings and Investment Accounts) 
 which are set out in a separate leaflet available upon request 
 and which also form part of the Terms and Conditions of this 
 Account. In the event of a conflict however, these specific 
 Account Terms and Conditions will prevail. Further details are 
 available upon request from the Society. Darlington Building 
 Society subscribes to The Banking Code. 
 
 
 
 A 
 cash payment equivalent to 1% of the average total balance in 
 all 'Pool Supporter Accounts throughout that year, and remaining 
 open on 30th June, will be paid to Hartlepool United Football 
 Club annually. The Society reserves the right to vary the rate 
 of interest or payment made to Hartlepool United Football Club, 
 or the manner in which they are calculated at any time without 
 notice. Such changes will be notified as soon as it is practicable 
 to do so through one or more of the following means: Advertisement 
 in local newspaper(s), Posters and/or leaflets displayed in 
 branches, Personal notice. 
 
 
 
 Darlington 
 Building Society is a participant in the Financial Services Compensation Scheme established under the Financial Services and Markets Act 2000.
Payments under the Scheme are limited to a maximum of £31,700, that is 100% of the first £2,000 of an investor's total shares and/or deposits in a society, and 90% of the next £33,000.
 Most investors are covered, including individuals and small firms. A small number of categories of shares and deposits are not covered. Although most share and deposits are denominated 
in sterling, all other currencies are covered. Further details are available on request 
 from the Society.
