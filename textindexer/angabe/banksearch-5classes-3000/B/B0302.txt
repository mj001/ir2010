 	Endowment Example: 
	 - 
 	 TYPICAL EXAMPLE-A 25 year interest only mortgage of £30,000 on a purchase price of £40,000 assuming a completion date of 30th April 2002 monthly mortgage payments are 36 payments of £137.25 followed by 264 payments of £140.00. Total gross amount payable of £72,415.51 includes application fee of £300.00, valuation fee of £105.00, sealing fee of £75.00, deeds production fee of £30.00, chaps fee of £25.00 capital repayment of £30,000 and accrued interest of £4.51.The example has been calculated using a rate of 5.49% for 36 months and then a rate of 5.6% for the remaining term APR 5.80% variable. The Society’s Standard Variable Rate will apply after the initial fixed rate period. A charge will be made if the mortgage is redeemed within 3 years of completion equal to 6 months interest. In this example the charge would be £840.00. No charge will be levied if the loan is repaid due to the death of a borrower.

Monthly mortgage payments are 36 payments of £186.19 followed by 264 payments of £188.02. Total gross amount payable of £56,854.63 includes application fee of £300.00, valuation fee of £105.00, sealing fee of £75.00, deeds production fee of £30.00, chaps fee of £25.00 and accrued interest of £4.51. The example has been calculated using a rate of 5.49% for 36 months and then a rate of 5.6% for the remaining term APR 6.0% variable. The Society’s Standard Variable Rate will apply after the initial fixed rate period. A charge will be made if the mortgage is redeemed within 3 years of completion equal to 6 months interest. In this example the charge would be £840.00. No charge will be levied if the loan is repaid due to the death of a borrower.
