	NOTES
Refers to all TMB products unless expressly stated 
	
	
	 Assessable Income for Full Status Mortgages - the basic salary/wage plus all of any guaranteed additional income e.g.commission/overtime/bonus and up to one half of any other regular and non guaranteed income is taken into account for each applicant. 
	
	
	
	 (1) For loans exceeding £500,000 contact your local Business Development Manager.
	 (2)HPA cut-in @70%on standard full status products for loans over £125,000.
	 (3) Income for employed applicants should be UK taxable and derived from stable permanent employment,i.e.in current job for at least 6 months at time of application, in permanent employment for the last successive 12 months,with a maximum of 2 employers in the last 12 months.Other sources of income may be acceptable.
	 (4) Minimum assessable income for primary applicants is £10,000 pa and £16,000 pa for joint applicants.
	 (5) Self-employed applicants-refer to individual product criteria regarding number of years trading. For full-status products,last 2 years trading accounts signed by a suitably qualified accountant/auditor are required.(ACA,ACCA,CIMA,CIPFA,AAPA,AAT,IFA).
	 (6) Applicants must be minimum age 20 at time of application (age 25 for Ten to Let).Primary applicant maximum age limit 70 at the end of the Mortgage term, provided the introducer can provide evidence of ability to repay after retirement.
	 (7) It is the borrowers responsibility to ensure suitable arrangements are in place to repay the loan on or before the end of the mortgage term and a suitable life assurance policy to cover the loan,whilst not compulsory,is strongly recommended.
	 (8) Advances available on properties in England,Wales,Mainland Scotland and Northern Ireland.
	 (9) Further advances will only be considered after 6 months satisfactory account conduct.
	 (10) All above apply subject to assessment as each application is individually underwritten.
	 (11) As part of our ongoing commitment to your client a telephone call will be made to them prior to any offer being issued.
