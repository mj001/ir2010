100% Mortgage Plan - Available to NUT members buying their first home only
-Teachers Building Society (TBS) to provide a mortgage equivalent to 75% of the 
 purchase price or valuation, whichever is the lower (APR 5.9% variable), with 
 the London Mortgage Company (LMC) providing a top-up loan for the remaining 
 25% (APR 9.0% variable)

-No application fee

-No Higher Lending Charge
