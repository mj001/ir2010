 Buying a property for investment purposes 
 is becoming increasingly popular. If you are considering buying a 
 residential property which you intend to let either to a family or 
 students, we offer an extremely competitive package. Up to 80% of 
 the purchase price/value can be borrowed and there is no minimum loan 
 that must be taken out to assist in the purchase.
