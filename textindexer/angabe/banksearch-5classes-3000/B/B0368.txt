 Terms & Conditions
 
 Typical Example
 A loan of £45,000 on a Interest only 
 basis secured on a property over 25 years on a valuation of £50,000. 
 36 monthly regular payments of £149.38, 40 monthly regular payments 
 of £214.74, 224 monthly regular payments of £199.81 and a 
 final payment of £45,000. Total gross amount payable £104,781. 
 Example assumes the Discount rate of 3.99% will apply for 36 months. The 
 Society's appropriate standard variable base interest rate, currently 
 5.74% will apply until 1 July 2008. The Society's appropriate loyalty 
 interest rate, currently 5.34% will apply for the remainder of the loan 
 term. (THE APR FOR THE LOAN WILL BE 5.3%). The loan is assumed to complete 
 on 15 February 2002. The total amount payable includes £146.88 solicitor's 
 charge, £25 CHAPS charge, £125 Completion charge, £15.90 
 per month for 36 months Buildings and Contents premium, £40 redemption 
 seal charge and assumes accrued interest in the first month of £76.66 
 (gross) is paid with the first full regular monthly payment. If the loan 
 is repaid in full, or in part (other than regular payments) before the 
 third anniversary following completion of the loan, the Society will charge 
 an amount equivalent to 5% of the sum repaid. Rates shown are variable.
