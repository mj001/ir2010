 Typical Example
 A loan of £45,000 on a Interest only basis secured 
 on a property over 25 years on a valuation of £50,000. 36 monthly 
 regular payments of £170.31, 36 monthly regular payments of £214.74, 
 228 monthly regular payments of £199.81 and a final payment of £45,000. 
 Total gross amount payable £104,684. Example assumes the Discount 
 rate of 4.55% will apply for 36 months. The Society's appropriate standard 
 variable base interest rate, currently 5.74% will apply until 1 July 2008. 
 The Society's appropriate loyalty interest rate, currently 5.34% will 
 apply for the remainder of the loan term. (THE APR FOR THE LOAN WILL BE 
 5.4%). The loan is assumed to complete on 15 June 2002. The total amount 
 payable includes £146.88 solicitor's charge, £40 redemption 
 seal charge and assumes accrued interest in the first month of £78.07 
 (gross) is paid with the first full regular monthly payment. If the loan 
 is repaid in full, or in part (other than regular payments) before the 
 first anniversary following completion of the loan, the Society will charge 
 an amount equivalent to 5% of the sum repaid, thereafter, If the loan 
 is repaid in full, or in part (other than regular payments) before the 
 second anniversary following completion of the loan, the Society will 
 charge an amount equivalent to 4% of the sum repaid, thereafter, If the 
 loan is repaid in full, or in part (other than regular payments) before 
 the third anniversary following completion of the loan, the Society will 
 charge an amount equivalent to 3% of the sum repaid Rates shown are variable.
