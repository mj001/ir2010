 Terms & Conditions
 
 Typical Example
 A loan of £45,000 on an Interest 
 only basis secured on a property over 25 years on a valuation of £50,000. 
 12 monthly regular payments of £111.99, 12 monthly regular payments 
 of £149.38, 12 monthly regular payments of £186.74, 40 monthly 
 regular payments of £214.74, 224 monthly regular payments of £199.81 
 and a final payment of £45,000. Total gross amount payable £104,189. 
 Example assumes the Discount rates of 2.99% will apply for 12 months, 
 then 3.99% for a further 12 months and then 4.99% will apply for a further 
 12 months. The Society's appropriate standard variable base interest rate, 
 currently 5.74% will apply until 1 July 2008. The Society's appropriate 
 loyalty interest rate, currently 5.34% will apply for the remainder of 
 the loan term. (THE APR FOR THE LOAN WILL BE 5.3%). The loan is assumed 
 to complete on 15 February 2002. The total amount payable includes £146.88 
 solicitor's charge, £25 CHAPS charge, £195 loan arrangement 
 charge, £40 redemption seal charge and assumes accrued interest 
 in the first month of £57.44 (gross) is paid with the first full 
 monthly regular payment. If the loan is repaid in full, or in part (other 
 than regular payments) before the second anniversary following completion 
 of the loan, the Society will charge an amount equivalent to 5% of the 
 sum repaid, thereafter, if the loan is repaid in full, or in part (other 
 than regular payments) before the fourth anniversary following completion 
 of the loan, the Society will charge an amount equivalent to 4% of the 
 sum repaid, thereafter, if the loan is repaid in full, or in part (other 
 than regular payments) before the fifth anniversary following completion 
 of the loan, the Society will charge an amount equivalent to 3% of the 
 sum repaid. Rates shown are variable.
