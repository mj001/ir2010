Higher Lending 
 Charge 
 This charge is applicable for mortgages in excess of 80% of the 
 purchase price or value (whichever the lower). The charge can be 
 added to the mortgage provided that the total loan to value ratio 
 does not exceed 97.5%.

All mortgage products are available to applicants who are either 18

 on the mortgage or any other loan secured on it.
