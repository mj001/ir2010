 	 Total protection for your mortgage payments against accident, illness and unemployment at no extra cost for the first 6 months. For full details on eligibility and benefits see separate leaflet. 
	 
	
	 
 	MIG: 
	 - 
 	 The Mortgage Indemnity Guarantee Premium may be added to the loan for new mortgages provided the total indebtedness does not exceed 95% of property value. However for mortgages up to and including 90% loan to value (LTV) the society will pay the premium

 	Endowment Example: 
	 - 
 	 TYPICAL EXAMPLE-A 25 year interest only mortgage of £30,000 on a purchase price of £40,000 assuming a completion date of 31st December 2001. Monthly mortgage payments are 300 payments of £117.50. Total gross amount payable of £65,483.86 includes application fee of £100.00, chaps fee of £25.00, sealing fee of £75.00, deeds production fee of £30.00, capital repayment of £30,000 and accrued interest of £3.86. This example has been calculated using a rate of 4.7% which is a discount of 0.9% from the Society's Standard Variable Rate which is currently 5.6%APR 4.8%variable. A redemption charge equal to 3 months interest at the prevailing Standard Variable Rate will be made if the loan is redeemed during the first 12 months. In this example the charge would be £420. The loan is fully portable. No charge will be made if the loan is redeemed due to the death of a borrower. Underpayments and payment holidays are only allowed to the value of previous over payments The cost of a basic mortgage valuation will be repaid by the Society on completion. A payment of £250.00 will be made on completion to applicants who are remortgaging
