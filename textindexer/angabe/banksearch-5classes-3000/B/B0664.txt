 Discounts Available
 
Advances up to 90% of the value of the property - up to 0.75%
 discount for 24 months. See below for recovery of discount and redemption interest.
 
After the discount period, the mortgage will revert to the Society's
 standard variable rate.
 
Redemption Interest
 
Redemption interest will not be charged in the event of early
 repayment of the mortgage for all mortgage offers issued after 26th November 2001.
 
Recovery Of Discount
 
All discount received will become repayable if the mortgage account
 is redeemed within the initial 2 year discount period.

The Society reserves the right to vary the quoted interest rate by
 giving notice in accordance with the Mortgage Conditions.
