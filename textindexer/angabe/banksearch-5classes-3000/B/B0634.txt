Mortgage Account - House Purchase
Mortgage Account - House Purchase
For borrowers purchasing a house for £80,000 and applying for an interest only mortgage of £50,000 over 25 years to be secured on the property.
The Mortgage rate of interest (variable) until 30th June 2003 is 4.00% APR 5.3%. The mortgage rate of interest (variable) from 1st July 2003 until 31st December 2009 is 4.75% APR 5.3%. From 1st January 2010 and for the remainder of the mortgage term, the Mortgage rate of interest (variable) will be the Society’s Standard Variable Rate, currently 5.55% APR 5.8%.
The total amount payable would be £116,455.13 (including the capital sum which would need to be repaid, as well as estimated fees and charges of £231.35). 
Assuming completion of the loan on 1st July 2002 there would be 11 monthly payments of £166.67, then 78 monthly payments of £197.92, followed by 211 monthly payments of £231.25. 
The APR is variable. If the loan is repaid in full before 31st August 2004, an early repayment charge of £500 will be payable.
Flexible Mortgage Account - Additional Borrowing
For borrowers deciding to draw an additional amount of £10,000 on a repayment basis over 6 years to be secured on the property.
The Flexible Mortgage rate of interest for the duration of the term is variable and currently 4.75% APR 5.0%. 
The total amount payable on the additional loan would be £11,549.82.
Assuming the full amount of £10,000 is borrowed on completion of the loan on 1st July 2002 the minimum monthly repayment needed to repay the loan by the end of the term would be £159.89. 
The APR is variable.
