 ADDITIONS & WITHDRAWALS
 
 Providing £25,000 remains, two withdrawals of up to £5,000 can be made each calendar year without notice or loss of interest. Withdrawals of at least £100 may be made without loss of interest subject to 180 days written notice or, on demand, subject to the loss of 180 days interest on the amount withdrawn.
You can add to the account at any time with further investments of at least £500 as long as the issue remains open. Further investments will not be allowed after this issue is closed.
