	Fund Objective 
	
	
	
	
- The fund aims to provide investors with a balanced spread of investments for long term capital appreciation by seeking well managed companies primarily in the UK where superior potential may be found
	
	 
	 
	
	Investment Policy 
	
	
	
	
- The growth objective is pursued by investing in companies within a sector/thematic framework. The process is essentially bottom up, the criteria for which includes earnings growth strong cash flow barriers to entry and quality of management. The portfolio will be spread between large, medium, and small companies.
	
	 
	 
	
	Fund Facts 
	
	
	
	
- Invests wholly in the ABN AMRO UK Growth Fund, an authorised unit trust
	
	
- Unit trust launched 23rd December 1986
	
	
- Pension fund launched 2nd May 2000
	
	
- Unit trust has a S&P Fund Research Rating of frAAA and a Micropal Star rating of 5 stars
