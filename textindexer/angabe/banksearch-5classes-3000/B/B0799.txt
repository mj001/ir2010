 Job
 Title: Assistant Manager 
 Grade:
 5/6 
 
 
 Department:
 Sales 
 Reports
 to: Branch Manager/City Business
 Manager 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Main
 purpose of job
 
 
 
 
 
 
- 
 
To support the Branch
 Manager by conducting mortgage interviews,
 and provide supervision, training and
 coaching to the team.
 
 
- 
 
To ensure sufficient leads
 are generated in respect of regulated sales
 and lending, including unsecured lending,
 also to sell traditional non-regulated
 investment products and general insurance in
 line with personal objectives and Delivering
 Excellence.
 
 
- 
 
Achieve Society’s
 Customer Service standards in all areas.
 
 
- 
 
Ensure effective
 administration in all areas and full
 compliance with Society audit requirements.
 
 
- 
 
To deputise in the absence
 of the Branch Manager/City Business Manager.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Key
 Accountables
 
 
 
 
 
 
- 
 
Deal effectively with
 initial mortgage enquiries up to and
 including mortgage interviewing.
 
 
- 
 
Supervising and monitoring
 the performance of colleagues.
 
 
- 
 
Providing coaching and
 feedback to colleagues.
 
 
- 
 
Assisting the Branch
 Manager in developing the business
 performance of the branch.
 
 
- 
 
Achieving personal targets
 by generating sufficient quality referrals
 for mortgage, investment and regulated
 products.
 
 
- 
 
Ensure personal sales of
 mortgage, investment and insurance products
 are in line with targets set.
 
 
- 
 
Ensure there is a high
 level of customer service at all times by
 providing an efficient and accurate counter
 service to all customers.
 
 
- 
 
Ensure that all customers
 who could be suitable to receive financial
 advice, or requesting information on
 regulated products and mortgages, are
 referred to the relevant experienced or
 authorised staff using Delivering
 Excellence. Make appointments where
 necessary.
 
 
- 
 
Ensure that the
 administration for mortgage applications and
 investments and other general branch
 administration is completed in line with
 Society’s Customer Service standards.
 
 
- 
 
Ensure branch audit
 security and cash management requirements
 are adhered to.
 
 
- 
 
To carry out any other
 tasks or duties as required and instructed
 by your line manager from time to time.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Internal
 and external relationships
 
 
 
 
 
Internal: Branch
 colleagues, Retail Sales Manager, Branch
 Managers, Head Office Departments, Development
 Managers.
 
External: Members
 & Customers, Norwich Union and Professional
 Connections, Future Mortgages.
 
 
 
 
 
 
Responsibilities
 for Resources, Financial/Human/Physical
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
- 
 
Cash holdings within the
 branch
 
 
 
- 
 
Customer Sales Advisers
 
 
- 
 
Security of the branch and
 all equipment/cash/documentation.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Qualifications
 Skills & Experience
 
 
 
 
 
 
- 
 
Educated to ‘A’ level
 standard.
 
 
- 
 
Minimum 1 year sales
 experience, preferably in financial
 services.
 
 
- 
 
Must be CeMap qualified
 within 12 months of appointment to role.
 
 
- 
 
Requires a proven ability
 to supervise and coach other people.
