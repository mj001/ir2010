	 ComponentOne Studio for .NET 
	
 Overview 
	 
	
 
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
 
 
 
 
 	 	 
Been eagerly
anticipating tools for the Microsoft .NET Framework? We understand! Not only
have we come up with the tools you want, we've introduced a value-packed
subscription service (tools, upgrades and updates, new .NET releases, and e-mail
support) just for .NET developers like you.
ComponentOne Studio for .NET is a collection of eleven royalty-free runtime distribution client products developed for use in windows forms inside Visual Studio.NET. This product includes the latest ComponentOne Windows Form components for .NET, updates and upgrades, new software offerings, and e-mail support for one full year. ComponentOne Studio for .NET includes high-quality Windows Form components including grid components, reporting components, charting components, data components, and user-interface components.
We've combined these components into one "Studio subscription" to offer you unparalleled business value! Now you can purchase our entire line of powerful Windows Form products for .NET for the average price of a single product subscription! Take advantage of special introductory pricing with
savings of up to $1,000!
 
	
	
	 
	
	 
	
	 
	
	 
	
	 
	
	 
	
	 
	
	 
	
	 
	
	 
	
	 
	
	 
	
	 
	
	 
	 
	
	
	
	 
	
	 How does the Studio for .NET annual subscription work? 
	
	 
	
	 What do I receive? 
	
	 
	
	 
	
	 
	
	
	
	 
	
	 
	
	 
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	 Initial Kit - When you order ComponentOne Studio for .NET, you receive an initial package that includes a master CD. This master CD includes all ComponentOne Studio for .NET products listed below. It will also contain a unique key that registers all products included in ComponentOne Studio for .NET.
 Updates, Upgrades and Betas - We are commited to adding value to your subscription every quarter. We'll do this by adding new products, features, and functionality to our components. For 3 subsequent quearters following your subscription purchase, we will ship a subscription CD directly to you. You can also download the updated components directly from our website at any time.
 New Products - As a ComponentOne Studio for .NET subscriber, you will receive any new Windows Form components that we release during the time of your subscription. The unique key included with your initial kit, will register any new products that you are qualified to receive.
 Free Email and Online technical support - Your ComponentOne Studio for .NET purchase includes complimentary e-mail support by a dedicated team of friendly responsive Technical Support personnel. (telephone support is available for $200/year, if desired). In addition, you receive online support on our Web site in the form of FAQs, sample code, knowledge base, and peer-to-peer newsgroups.
 Documentation - Tools in ComponentOne Studio for .NET include thorough online Help and a Help PDF file that may be printed. Many also include demos, tutorials, and sample projects with source code. Printed manuals for ComponentOne Studio for .NET products are available for purchase directly from our distributor Edge Incorporated Online-store.
 Bonus CD - If you've purchased ComponentOne Studio for .NET in 2001 - you will receive a 4th quarterly CD at no additional charge! This is another way to thank you for your continued support.
 Renewals - After your susbcription expires, you can continue using the components that were released while your subscription was active. You can continue to develop and distribute your applications built using ComponentOne Studio for .NET products. You will need to renew your subscription to download new updates, upgrades, and products.
