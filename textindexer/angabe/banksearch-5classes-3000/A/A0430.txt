Use the links below to find out how to get the most out of cahoot. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Deposit money into all types of cahoot account by 
 Direct Debit, and into a cahoot current account by post or at most 
 post offices.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 in the post
 You'll be able to deposit cheques into your current account by posting 
 them, together with a completed paying-in slip, in a white pre-paid 
 cheque deposit envelope that we'll send you. Cheques take up to 
 six working days to clear. You'll always be able to order fresh 
 supplies of envelopes online if you run out. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 local post offices
 Deposit up to £10,000 cash per day into your cahoot current 
 account at most UK post offices, using a paying-in slip from your 
 paying-in book or your cheque book. Cash deposits can take up to 
 five working days to be credited to your account. To deposit cheques, 
 present them together with a completed paying-in slip in a green 
 cheque deposit envelope (we send you these when you open a cahoot 
 current account).
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Direct Debit
 You can set up a Direct Debit online when you apply and then use 
 it to deposit money into your accounts. You'll be able to access 
 your Direct Debit online through your secure banking homepage and 
 set it up to draw money into your accounts.
