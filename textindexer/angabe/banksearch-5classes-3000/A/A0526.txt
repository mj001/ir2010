 Your home is probably the single biggest investment 
 you make in your life. If your circumstances change, you need 
 to make sure your household is protected. The SELF ASSURANCE 
 Mortgage service will make sure your mortgage is paid if you 
 find yourself unable to do so.

 If you were to suffer the severe 
 set back of being diagnosed with a critical illness, mortgage 
 payments are an additional worry that you and those close to 
 you can do without. The SELF ASSURANCE Mortgage policy can help 
 you overcome the financial consequences of in the event of critical 
 illness or death and your mortgage payments could be settled 
 in full.
 
 In addition, your premiums will automatically be paid if you 
 cannot work due to sickness, accident or disability.
 
 This service is to be used only with a repayment mortgage; the 
 benefit decreases each year in line with a mortgage taken out 
 with an interest rate of 10%.
 
 You also have the option to take an added unemployment benefit. 
 This will pay you a monthly income to cover your mortgage payments 
 if you find yourself unemployed. The SELF ASSURANCE Mortgage 
 service also makes sure that your premiums are paid while you 
 are receiving the benefit.
