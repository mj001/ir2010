 ISAs (Individual Savings Accounts) offer a straightforward way to save up to £7,000
each tax year (until the end of the 2005-2006 tax year, after which this maximum may
change) without paying tax on the returns.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Enjoy the security you get from an ordinary bank or building society savings account, with
the added advantage of competitive rates of tax-free interest.
 
 
 All you need to open an Easy ISA is £10. Enjoy a choice of ways to operate your account and competitive rates of tax-free interest. 
 
 
 If you have a minimum of £3000 to invest and are happy to operate your account via the post, the Postal ISA offers competitive rates of tax-free interest.
