	Banking
	
	Classic account moving to Lloyds TSB Under 19's account OWN student account OWN graduate account
	
	 
	
	
	
	
	
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	
	 
	 
	
	
	
	
	
	
	We're here to make life easier while you're studying. Our OWN student account gives you free banking as long as you're within your overdraft limit, as well as: 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	
	 
	 
	
	
	
	
	
	
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	an interest free overdraft - up to £1,500 in each of your first 3 years, and up to £2,000 in years 4 and 5 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	 
	
	 commission free travel money - Traveller's Cheques and foreign currency 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	 
	
	a debit card is available once you are 18, which you can use to guarantee cheques up to £100, as a Cashpoint® card and wherever the VISA symbol is displayed (both within the UK and worldwide) 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	 
	
	Internet banking - access to your account 7 days a week, from 4am to midnight 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	 
	
	telephone banking - PhoneBank Express is open 24/7; once your account has been set up, you'll receive a pack to complete your registration 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	 
	
	a credit card - you can apply for an Asset credit card 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	 
	
	an expert student banking team - once you've opened your account, our dedicated OWN telephone team are available on 0845 3000 134 from 9am to 7pm, Monday to Friday to help you with any financial queries 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	
	 
	 
	
	
	
	
	
	
	am I eligible?
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	To be eligible for your OWN account, you must: 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	
	 
	 
	
	
	
	
	
	
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	be a permanent UK resident 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	 
	
	be studying full time on a course of at least 3 years in duration leading to an honours degree 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	 
	
	have an unconditional offer letter or a UCAS acceptance letter for a full time, 3 year minimum, honours degree course
