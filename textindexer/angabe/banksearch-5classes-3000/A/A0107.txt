 Benefits of Bank of Ireland Personal Loans 
 
 
 
 
 
- Low cost repayments
 
- Flexible amounts from £500 - £15,000 and choice of term - designed to meet customers needs 
 
- Fixed repayments make it easy to budget ahead
 
- Payment break option - up to 2 months a year when the customer doesn't need to make any payments 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Benefits of Payment Protection 
 
 
 
 
 
- Peace of mind for the customer
 
- Easy to arrange with no application form 
 
- Low cost for level of cover provided
 
- No medical required 
 
- No loading of premium
