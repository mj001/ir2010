Your Home
 
 
	
 
 
 
 
 
 
	 
Moving In
	 
Don't forget to tell people you have moved. There are a number of people you should tell, besides your friends, just a few are listed below:
- Banks - update accounts for all the family. We can make it easy for you. If you are a Customer of the Northern, simply let us know your new address.
If you have been wanting to review your banking, now is the time to do it, whether or not you are a Customer. The staff at your nearest Branch of the Northern can provide you with up to date information about the accounts and services we offer and help you tailor your banking to your current needs.
- Insurance Companies - house insurance (buildings and contents), personal liability, life assurance, private health insurance, car insurance. Update details for all members of the family.
- Social Security - age and invalid pensioners and family allowance recipients must notify the Department of Social Security of changes of address.
- Credit Cards - Store cards, Bank cards, Credit Cards etc. Allow plenty of advance warning to ensure that Bills are not sent to the wrong address, delaying payment and possibly costing you money.
- Car Registration - required by law.
- Driver's Licence - required by law.
- Electoral Register - required by law.
- Schools / Colleges - make arrangements well in advance for the transfer of your children from one school / college to another.
- Medical - notify your doctor.
- Dentist - notify your dentist.
- Electricity Board - notify when to switch off at old address and switch on at new address.
- Telephone - notify your telecommunications company well in advance of your move to ensure that the telephone will be connected when you move in.
- Post Office - redirect all mail for several months by notifying the Post Office of your change of address (there is a fee for this service). Note any correspondence sent to your old address and notify senders accordingly.
- Milk Delivery - cancel delivery or notify of new address, if in the same district.
- Newsagents - cancel newspapers and magazine deliveries or notify of new address, if in the same district.
- Correspondents - far away friends and members of the family, with whom you only have infrequent contact such as the exchange of Christmas Cards.
- Clubs and Associations - advise any club or association you belong to, be they professional, sporting or social.
- Subscriptions - magazines and journals you subscribe to.
- Consultants / Advisors - notify your Solicitor, accountant, financial advisor etc.
