These rates apply to the main Bank of Ireland Savings Accounts that are currently on offer.
 
	
	Interest rates may vary from time to time. 
	This notice lists current gross annual interest rates with effect from 26th October 2001. 
	Interest will be paid Net of Lower Rate Tax (currently 20%) or, where Bank of Ireland receives the relevant registration documentation, gross. 
	Interest earned on ISA and Tessa accounts is tax-free. 
	AER stands for Annual Equivalent Rate and illustrates what the interest rate would be if interest was paid and compounded each year. 
	Full account conditions and details of when interest is paid are available from any Bank of Ireland branch or by contacting our Customer Services Dept. 
	Freepost BEL 1732, Belfast, BT1 5BR (Freephone: 0800 833937). 
	Bank of Ireland subscribes to the Banking Code and is also a member of the Financial Ombudsman Service.
