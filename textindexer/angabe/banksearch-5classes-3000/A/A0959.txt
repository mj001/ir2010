 Direct Saver
 
 
 
 The 
 most advanced online savings account, the Direct Saver account, gives you 
 direct access to your savings day and night over the Internet and offers a 
 competitive rate. Why not 
 
 apply online now? 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Deposit Limits 
 
 
 Access restriction and charges 
 
 
 Interest 
 
 
 
 Minimum 
 £50
 Maximum £1m 
 
 Direct 
 access if you operate the account over the Internet by our Digital 
 Banking service (see below for details). 
 
 
 Competitive Rates - tiered and paid annually. Up to 4.00% gross/AER for balances of 
 £50,000 or over. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
- You can
 
 
 apply online and start saving now. 
 
- The
 interest rates for the Direct Saver account are 
 competitive, tiered and paid annually. Use our
 
 Savings Calculator to see how your savings can grow.
 
 
- Operating 
 your account online using 
 Digital Banking, 
 our free internet banking service, is 
 easy and it gives you direct access to your savings night and day, seven 
 days a week from your PC or Mac, your desktop or your laptop. Digital 
 Banking supports Microsoft® Internet Explorer (version 4 and above) or 
 Netscape Navigator (version 4.08 to 4.78 inclusive) on a PC with 
 Microsoft® Windows 95/98/2000/Me/XP or NT4 or an Apple Macintosh with 
 OS8.1 or above.
 
- You only 
 need a deposit of £50 to open a Direct Saver account.
 
 
- Using
 
 Digital Banking further deposits to your Direct Saver 
 account can be made by immediate automatic transfer from any Royal Bank of 
 Scotland account in your name or by Direct Debit from an account you hold 
 at other UK banks or building societies. Deposits from an account 
 at other UK banks or building societies will normally be in your Direct 
 Saver account in 3 working days after we receive transfer instructions and 
 available for withdrawal after 6 working days. The Direct Debit 
 transaction limit is £100,000. 
 
- 
 Withdrawals can be made without notice and without loss of interest using
 
 Digital Banking
 either to another Royal Bank account you 
 hold or by making a direct payment to other UK bank or building 
 society accounts. Withdrawals to an account at other UK banks or 
 building societies will normally reach the payee's account in 3 working 
 days. The direct payment daily limit is £5,000. 
 
- You can 
 also set up a standing order to help you begin saving 
 regularly &#150; this can be completed as part of the online application form.
 
 
- 
 Interest is paid gross if you are a non-taxpayer. Just complete 
 the Inland Revenue form which is part of the online application form.
 
 
- 
 Interest is calculated daily and paid 
 annually at the end of April. 
 
- If you 
 have any queries about the Direct Saver account call us on 0845 603 0733.
