	how to apply
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	To apply for an overdraft online, you need to be registered for our Internet banking service. Click here to apply for Internet Banking. 
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	If you are registered and require an overdraft click on the "apply now" button at the top of the page. Once you have logged in and selected the account for which you require an overdraft, select 'Overdrafts' from the left hand menu. 
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	Our overdrafts are free to set up and are charge and interest free under £10 and you can also protect your borrowing with our Personal overdraft protection. 
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	Alternatively, you can contact us via PhoneBank if you are registered, or contact your local Lloyds TSB branch.
