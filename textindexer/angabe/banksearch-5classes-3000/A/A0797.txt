Financing your travel
 
 
	
	
 
 
You know where you want to go, but financially you're 
 not sure how to get there? Here are a few ideas to help you on your 
 way: 
 
	
 
 
 
	
 
 Personal loans 
 
	
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Ideal if you want to borrow a set 
 amount for a set period of time 
 
 
 
 Borrow any amount from £500 to 
 £25,000 
 
 
 
 A choice of repayment periods are 
 available 
 
 
 
 Fixed monthly repayments 
 
 
 
 No security required 
 
 
 
Call 0800 
 130 130* for further information or to apply for a personal 
 loan.
 
 
	 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Overdrafts 
 
 
 
 Ideal if you want flexible, short term borrowing 
 
 
 
 You only plan to use it for six months or less 
 
 
 
 You can stay within your overdraft limit 
 
 
 
 You have a regular income to cover payments
