	E-Business Deposit Account
	
	This deposit account is deliberately simple and straightforward, making it incredibly easy to earn high rates of interest on your surplus funds day-to-day. It's an ideal way to earn more from your money.
	
	Guaranteed competitive rates of interest
	
	Highly attractive rates of interest are guaranteed with no restriction on when you can access your deposit.
Furthermore, Bank of Scotland offers an interest rate guarantee, so you can be confident that you are always earning competitive interest on your balance. Click here to find out more about our interest rate guarantee.
	
	Complete flexibility
	
	You will have complete control to move funds into and out of your account whenever you wish ensuring you are always maximising the interest you earn.
Inter-account transfers can be made quickly and easily via the Internet at any time, ensuring your funds are where you want them, when you want them.
Inter-account transfers take effect the same day provided they are instructed before 6pm on a business day. Transfers can also be instructed to a pre-selected account outside Bank of Scotland, but may take up to 5 working days.
