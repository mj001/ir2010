 AER: Annual Equivalent 
 Rate: This is a notional rate which illustrates what the gross 
 rate would be if interest were paid and compounded each year. 
 
 
 GROSS: The rate before 
 the deduction of tax applicable to interest on savings accounts.
 APR: Annual Percentage Rate. This is the total cost of credit, 
 including all interest and any other charges.
 EAR: Effective Annual Rate. This takes account of the interest 
 rate and how often interest is paid, and does not include any 
 fees or charges. 
 
 
 Tax free: Tax free means 
 free of Income Tax. 
 
 
 All rates are quoted per 
 annum, unless otherwise indicated, and are variable.
