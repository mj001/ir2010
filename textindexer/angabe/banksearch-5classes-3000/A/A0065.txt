 The small print
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
	 
	
	
	
	
	
	
	
	 
	
Whether you're looking to revamp your home, want to buy a new car or splash out on a once-in-a-lifetime holiday, a Halifax loan means that you can afford it.  You'll find a Halifax loan that's just right for you. Choose from:
- Personal Loan - loans available from £500 to £25,000 with rates starting at 7.9% APR and option of no monthly repayment for the first three months (typical APR 8.9%).
- Balance Transfer Loan - with a special introductory rate for the first six months from 4.9% AER, typical APR 10.9%, to pay off personal loans from other lenders. 
- HomeOwner Loan - secured on your home, it enables you to spread your payments over a longer period of time at a current rate of 5.8% APR (variable).
- Our Car Finance options - so that whether you're looking for a new or used car, you can find the best finance deals for you.
