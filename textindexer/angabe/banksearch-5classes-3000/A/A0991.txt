	As life changes, so will your financial needs. So it’s wise to always seek the best financial advice as your life develops. 
A Bristol & West Financial Planning Manager can offer you personal advice anytime you want - whether it’s on the type of investment product you need or the type of mortgage that’s best for you. All you need to do is contact them and they’ll be pleased to help. 
Whatever your requirements and circumstances, your Financial Planning Manager can provide you with the perfect solution. Here are some of the ways that they can help you with a financial health check: 
- Make the most of tax allowances that the government gives you. 
- Investigate the benefits of stockmarket linked investments. 
- Find the Bristol & West mortgage that best suits you. 
- Make sure you can afford your mortgage should you be unable to work or are made redundant. 
- Check if you can reduce any mortgage repayments you may have. 
- Make sure you have adequately protected yourself and your family against illness, disability or death. 
- Check how much provision you need to enjoy your retirement.
