	PC Spec 
	
	
	 
	
	
	 
	
	
	To run HOBS, your computer must have: 
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	 
	IBM compatibility with Pentium 120 processor 
	
	
	 
	
	
	 
	A hard disk, 3.5" floppy drive or CD-ROM and a mouse 
	
	
	 
	
	
	 
	32Mb or more of RAM 
	
	
	 
	
	
	 
	Microsoft Windows® 95, Windows® NT 4.0, Windows® 98 or Windows® 2000 operating system 
	
	
	 
	
	
	 
	Hayes® compatible modem 
	
	
	 
	
	
	 
	Serial COM port 
	
	
	 
	
	
	 
	Spare serial port for smart card reader 
	
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	HOBS Customer Tariff 
	
	
	 
	
	
	 
	
	
	There is a fixed monthly subscription for access to HOBS facilities. This is currently: 
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	 
	
	
	 
	Business HOBS £30 
	
	
	 
	
	
	 
	Corporate HOBS £70 
	
	
	 
	
	
	 	
	
	This covers all Bank of Scotland accounts which you choose to operate on HOBS. This charge includes an installation pack containing software disks or CD-ROM, a set-up guide, computer-based tutorial software, comprehensive help files and on-going support from our telephone Help Desk. There is a charge made for the supply of Smart Cards and Card Readers which are required to authorise instructions.
 Telephone Call Charges: the connection to HOBS is via your telephone line and these calls are charged at local telephone rates.
