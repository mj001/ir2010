	Our Services
	
	Internet banking telephone banking the Post Office service how to apply Personal Choice Lloyds TSB international banking
	
 
	
	 
	
	
	
	
	
	
	 
	 
	
	
	
	
	
	 
	 
	
	
	
	
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	
Internet banking gives you access to your account at any time between 4am and midnight, 7 days a week, offering a convenient and secure way to bank online. This service is free. All you pay for is access to the internet. 
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	With Internet banking you can: 
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	
	 
	 
	
	
	
	
	
	 
	 
	
	
	
	
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	check your balance and the money going in and out of your account 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	 
	
	transfer money between your own accounts or to another person with a UK bank account 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	 
	
	pay bills, such as your credit card and utility bills 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	 
	
	set up, change and delete your standing orders 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	 
	
	cancel Direct Debits 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	 
	
	print your statement or download it into a financial software package 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	 
	
	open an Online Saver account and transfer money to and from it instantly 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	 
	
	apply for a new or increased overdraft 
	
	
	
	
	 
	
	
	
	 
	
	order foreign currency and Travellers Cheques
